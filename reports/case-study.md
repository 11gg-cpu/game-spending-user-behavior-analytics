# Case Study

## Objective

Understand how different game mechanics influence engagement and spending behavior.

## User Segments

| Segment | Behavior | Product Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Competitive Core | Frequent ranked play, high session depth | Motivated by mastery and status |
| Cosmetic Spender | Purchases skins or visual items | Values identity and social display |
| Event-Driven Returner | Returns during limited events | Sensitive to seasonal campaigns |
| Casual Explorer | Low frequency, broad trial behavior | Needs lower-friction onboarding |

## Suggested Metrics

- Weekly active days.
- Session frequency.
- Purchase count and purchase category.
- Event participation rate.
- Ranked progression or achievement milestones.

## Product Recommendations

- Use limited-time events to reactivate casual and returning users.
- Keep cosmetic monetization tied to identity, not pay-to-win advantage.
- Design battle-pass progress to reward both frequent and moderate users.
