# Data Output Summary

## Key Segments

- Competitive Core: high session depth, status-driven retention.
- Cosmetic Spender: moderate engagement but higher spend, identity-driven purchases.
- Event-Driven Returner: responds to limited-time events and seasonal missions.
- Casual Explorer: low frequency, needs simple onboarding and clear value.

## Product Interpretation

The most useful monetization design is not only discounting. For long-term retention, the product should connect spending options to identity, progression, and social display while avoiding pay-to-win pressure.
